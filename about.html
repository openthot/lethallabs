The Mission.

Who We Are

Lethal Labs was founded on a simple principle: software should not only work flawlessly, but it should feel incredible to use. We are a collective of hardcore engineers, designers, and visionaries dedicated to advancing the open-source ecosystem while delivering state-of-the-art SaaS solutions.

Operating predominantly on GitHub, our firm embraces a culture of extreme transparency, rigorous code reviews, and constant iteration. The "Lethal" in our name represents our approach to technical debt—we eliminate it ruthlessly.

Core Values
